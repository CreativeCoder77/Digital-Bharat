About Our Mission

Driving transformation through nine key areas, fostering inclusive growth and innovation.

Digital Bharat Foundation is committed to transforming rural India through innovative digital solutions. Our mission is to bridge the technological gap, empower communities, and foster sustainable development in every village. We believe that access to digital resources is a fundamental right that can unlock immense potential.

We focus on key areas like digital literacy, accessible healthcare, renewable energy, and smart agriculture, leveraging cutting-edge technology to create lasting impact. Our approach is collaborative, working closely with local communities, government bodies, and volunteers to ensure our initiatives are effective and sustainable.

Report a Problem

Facing an issue in your village or personally? Let us know, and we'll connect you with the right solutions.

How This Form Helps You

In many parts of India, especially in rural and semi-urban areas, people often face challenges that go unreported simply because they don’t know how or where to raise their concerns. This form bridges that gap. Whether you're dealing with irregular electricity, damaged roads, lack of sanitation, water shortages, poor internet connectivity, or unresponsive local officials — this is your opportunity to speak up and be heard.

Once you submit the form, your report is analyzed and routed to the relevant authorities, development organizations, or government helplines that can take action. If your issue affects your entire village, you can mark it as such — helping us escalate it for faster resolution. We're building a smarter, more connected Bharat where every citizen, regardless of location, has access to the tools of digital empowerment.

By filling out this form, you're not just reporting a problem — you're helping create a database of real-world issues that can influence policy-making, improve service delivery, and hold systems accountable. You’re making your voice count. Together, we can bring visibility to local problems and help build a nation where progress reaches every corner.
