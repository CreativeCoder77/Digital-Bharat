The Vision of Digital India

Digital India is a flagship program of the Government of India with a vision to transform India into a digitally empowered society and knowledge economy. It encompasses three key vision areas.

Digital Infrastructure

Providing high-speed internet as a core utility, creating secure and stable digital platforms for citizens.

Governance & Services

Making government services digitally accessible, efficient, and transparent for all citizens.

Digital Empowerment

Promoting digital literacy, universal digital resources, and collaborative digital platforms for citizens.

A Timeline of Digital Transformation

Key milestones and achievements shaping India's digital journey.

2009: Aadhaar Launched

UIDAI established to issue Aadhaar numbers, revolutionizing identity in India.

2014: Digital India Program

Launched to transform India into a digitally empowered society and knowledge economy.

2016: UPI Launched

Unified Payments Interface revolutionizes digital payments in India.

2017: DigiLocker Integration

DigiLocker becomes widely integrated, promoting paperless governance.
