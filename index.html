timeline A Timeline of Digital Transformation

From foundational steps to revolutionary milestones, India's digital journey has been transformative.

2009: Aadhaar Launched

The UIDAI was established, rolling out Aadhaar. As of April 2025, 142 crore Aadhaar IDs have been generated, forming the bedrock of digital identity.

2015: Digital India Program Inaugurated

The national agenda for digital transformation was set. DigiLocker was launched, reaching 53.92 crore users by June 2025.

2016: UPI Revolutionizes Payments

Unified Payments Interface (UPI) launched, enabling real-time mobile payments. In April 2025, UPI recorded 1,867.7 crore transactions.

2020: NEP & Digital Learning Push

The National Education Policy (NEP) 2020 emphasized technology in education. The DIKSHA platform saw massive growth, recording over 1.85 billion learning sessions by Dec 2020.

2022: 5G & ONDC Rollout

5G services launched, ushering in an era of high-speed connectivity. Open Network for Digital Commerce (ONDC) began democratizing e-commerce.

Ongoing: BharatNet Expansion

As of January 2025, BharatNet has connected over 2.18 lakh Gram Panchayats with high-speed internet, laying nearly 6.92 lakh km of optical fibre cable to bridge the rural-urban digital divide.

AI Advancement Timeline

2018: #AIforAll Strategy

NITI Aayog releases the National Strategy for Artificial Intelligence, setting a vision for inclusive AI development.

2019-Present: Sectoral Integration

Rapid acceleration in AI startups and government adoption in agriculture (Kisan e-Mitra), education, and healthcare.

2023: India AI Mission

Launch of the $1.2 billion mission to boost AI capabilities, provide GPU access, and establish Centres of Excellence.

Future Horizons

Deepening AI Integration

Sophisticated AI in smart cities, personalized education, and advanced diagnostics with a focus on ethical guidelines.

Quantum Computing

Investing in research to revolutionize fields like drug discovery, materials science, and complex data analysis.

Global Digital Leadership

Sharing India's successful Digital Public Infrastructure (DPI) model with the world to foster global empowerment.
